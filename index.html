How to rent a car?

Book

To rent a car leave a request on the site or call/write to the messenger. Your personal manager will contact you and clarify all the details.

Prepare documents

For reservation and drawing up a contract, you need a passport photo, driver's license, location or hotel name, as well as a phone number for communication.

Get a car

We will bring the car to you at the hotel or at the airport and sign the contract. In addition to payment, it will be necessary to make a deposit, which will be returned upon delivery of the car.

Are car insured?

Yes, all of our cars are insured. When you rent a car in our company, you can rest assured that the car is insured.

What should I do if I have an accident?

You should call the support service at the number specified in the contract, or by phone at +66 99 149 4737. The operator will tell you what to do. You can also contact us via WhatsApp or Telegram by phone number +66 99 149 4737.

What do I need to know about the road rules in Thailand?
  1. In Thailand, left-hand traffic;
  2. There is no such thing as a gratuitous stop in Thailand;
  3. Very often, the police conduct special operations to search for drunk drivers and drivers without a license. With such a raid, all cars in a row are stopped. For driving under the influence of alcohol, a fine of 20,000 baht or a prison term follows;
  4. Overtaking or outrunning another car is allowed only on the right side.
  5. Any unregulated intersection is regarded as equivalent.
  6. At a red traffic light, a left turn is allowed, but only in the absence of a forbidding sign and giving way to other road users.
  7. Usually, the blinking of the headlights means that the driver wants to drive first.
  8. Speed limits: in the locality - 60 km/h, outside the locality - 90 km/h, on the highway - 110 km/h.
  9. Do not forget that any instructions from a police officer are a priority and must be carried out without complaints.
